Italian‐American Heritage Month

And the question is: Why has it not been solidified sooner? This is something I wonder every day. As a fellow Italian-American, I have an internal urge to want to spread recognition and awareness. I take great pride in my culture and knowing that its one that not many learn about, or celebrate, makes me want to be one of the people that brings light to our beautiful, hardworking, and family-orientated culture.

Italian-American Heritage and Culture should be recognized and acknowledged by the people of the United States. October is National Italian Heritage and Culture Month. Curated to salute the Italian and Italian-American community and to show appreciation for their culture and heritage which has immensely enhanced the lives of the people of the United States as well as globally. Therefore, it would only be proper to observe October as Italian Culture and Heritage Month.

Italian Heritage and Culture Month was just recognized on January 1, 2022, as a celebratory occasion, but only in Ohio.

On October 11, 2022, U.S. Congressman Josh Gottheimer (NJ-5) is working on resolving this issue with the U.S. House of Representatives.
